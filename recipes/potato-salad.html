Oma's Potato Salad

Description

Oma's potato salad is a simple, vinegar-based dish that reminds me of home. The most important finishing ingredients are bacon bits and chives.

Ingredients

  • gold potatoes
  • Essex vinegar
  • white onion
  • bacon bits
  • chives
  • beef bouillon cube
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil gold potatoes until tender enough to peel, about 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, finely dice one half of white onion.
  3. Boil a small amount of water and dissolve beef bouillon cube into boiling water.
  4. Add approximately 2 tablespoons of Essex vinegar to boiling water.
  5. Taste sauce, add additional vinegar or water to taste.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Drain gold potatoes and peel.
  8. Slice gold potatoes into bite size chunks; pour sauce over potatoes in bowl.
  9. Add bacon bits and chives to taste right before serving.
  10. Refrigerate leftovers (if you have any!)
