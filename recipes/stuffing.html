Stuffing

Description

A Thanksgiving dinner staple, this stuffing recipe comes straight from my Oma's kitchen.

Ingredients

  • white onion
  • loaf of bread
  • celery
  • stick of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • milk
  • turkey liver
  • poultry seasoning
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut up entire loaf of bread into cubes, dry for 1-2 days.
  2. Boil turkey liver, neck, and giblets in pot of water.
  3. Melt one stick of butter in small pot.
  4. Add 2 handfuls of finely chopped celery to melted butter, saute for approximately 10 minutes.
  5. Add 1/2 cup finely diced white onion to pot, saute for approximately 5 minutes.
  6. Pour mixture over dried bread and mix.
  7. Add poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste.
  8. Finely chop liver and add to stuffing.
  9. Add 2 eggs, some broth from the turkey innards, and milk to stuffing until moist.
  10. Stuff turkey and cook as directed.
